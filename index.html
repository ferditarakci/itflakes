Brand Story

It all began with a toothache followed by a routine visit to the dentist’s office.It was a spark, an idea, based on a simple observation over the counter as the convergence of one process to another, and one form (paper) to another (electronic), led to office frustration and confusion.

This should be effortless, and it should be fun. Thus inspired one person who inspired yet another and a company was born among techies who wanted to share their zest and excitement for technology. This single moment united a team who believed in the power of information technology and automation to enrich our lives, excite users, wondrously unfold a patient’s life click by click, magically transform to-do lists into ‘done’, and make work and life exceptionally fun.

The beauty of IT continually marvels us, and we strive to be recognized as the company that reignites that initial excitement you felt about technology. We want to simplify your every day.
